    • WIC Belgrade22: Who?, What?, When? Guide

    The World Athletics Indoor Championships in Belgrade, Serbia are just around the corner after a four year hiatus due to the COVID-19 pandemic. In this in-depth guide, we will go through the who? what? and when? of each event.

    Men's 60m

    Dates: Saturday 19 March
    World Record: 6.34 (Christian Coleman, USA, 18 Feb 2018)
    Championship Record:6.37 (Christian Coleman, USA, 03 Mar 2018)
    World Lead: 6.45 (Terrence Jones, BAH, 15 Jan 2022) / (Christian Coleman, USA, 27 Feb 2018) Previous Champion: Christian Coleman, USA (6.37)

    Men's 400m

    Dates: Friday 18 March (Heats & Semi-Finals) - Saturday 19 March (Final)
    World Indoor Record: 44.57 (Kerron Clement, USA, 12 Mar 2018)
    Championship Record:45.11 (Nery Brenes, CRC, 10 Mar 2012)
    World Indoor Lead: 44.83 (Randolph Ross, USA, 11 Feb 2022)
    Previous Champion: Pavel Maslák, CZE (45.47)

    Men's 800m

    Dates: Friday 18 March (Heats) - Saturday 19 March (Final)
    World Indoor Record: 1:42.67 (Wilson Kipketer, KEN, 09 Mar 1997)
    Championship Record: 1:42.67 (Wilson Kipketer, KEN, 09 Mar 1997)
    World Indoor Lead: 1:45.12 (Mariano García, ESP, 06 Feb 2022)
    Previous Champion: Adam Kszczot, POL (1:47.47)

    Men's 1500m

    Dates: Friday 18 March (Heats) - Saturday 19 March (Final)
    World Indoor Record: 3:30.60 (Jakob Ingebrigtsen, NOR, 17 Feb 2022) PENDING RATIFICATION
    Championship Record: 3:33.77 (Haile Gebrselassie, ETH, 07 Mar 1999)
    World Indoor Lead: 3:30.60 (Jakob Ingebrigtsen, NOR, 17 Feb 2022)
    Previous Champion: Samuel Tefera, ETH (3:58.19)

    Men's 3000m

    Dates: Friday 18 March (Heats) - Sunday 20 March (Final)
    World Indoor Record: 7:24.90 (Daniel Komen, KEN, 06 Feb 1998)
    Championship Record: 7:34.71 (Haile Gebrselassie, ETH, 09 Mar 1997)
    World Indoor Lead: 7:26.20 (Berihu Aregawi, ETH, 28 Jan 2022)
    Previous Champion: Yomif Kejelcha, ETH (8:14.41)

    Men's 60m Hurdles

    Dates: Sunday 20 March
    World Record: 7.29 (Grant Holloway, USA, 24 Feb 2021)
    Championship Record: 7.34 (Dayron Robles, CUB, 14 Mar 2010)
    World Lead: 7.35 (Grant Holloway, USA, 17 2022)
    Previous Champion: Andrew Pozzi, GBR (7.46)
